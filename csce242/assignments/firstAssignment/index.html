Transformers

Transformers are iconic robots known for their ability to transform between different forms They typically shift from humanoid robots to vehicles like cars, trucks, or planes.
These characters are split into the Autobots, who are the heroes, and the Decepticons, who are the villains. The concept of Transformers first emerged in the 1980s with a toy line created by Hasbro, which quickly led to an animated TV series and comic books. Each Transformer has a unique personality, abilities, and transformation, making them distinct and memorable.


The Autobots are led by Optimus Prime. The decepticons are led by Megatron.
The epic struggle between these two factions is filled with lots of action and plenty of memorbale moments. The battles between these robots also tell important stories on traits like leadership and humility
